T01Thermal Performance熱舒適性能必備 Precondition
中文重點 · 快速學習
建立多數使用者可接受的 indoor thermal environment，讓 regularly occupied spaces 在溫度、濕度、風速與平均輻射溫度等條件下維持舒適。此 feature 是 Thermal Comfort 的 precondition，重點在於用 PMV 條件與定期監測確認空間不是只靠設計宣稱，而是能持續提供可接受熱環境。
  • 適用 For All Spaces except Commercial Kitchen Spaces。
  • Option 1: Performance verified environmental conditions，mechanically conditioned regularly occupied spaces 需符合 PMV 條件。
  • PMV Range +/- 0.5：For at least 90% Occupied Hours，且 At least 90% Regularly Occupied Spaces。
  • PMV Range +/- 1.0：For at least 98% Occupied Hours，且 At least 95% Regularly Occupied Spaces。
  • Part 2 適用 For All Spaces except Dwelling Units；dry-bulb temperature、relative humidity、air speed（only for projects that use elevated air speed method）與 mean radiant temperature 需監測。
  • 監測頻率為 intervals no less than twice a year，including once in June, July or August and once in December, January or February，並 annually through the WELL digital platform 提交結果。

T02Verified Thermal Comfort熱舒適驗證加分 Optimization
中文重點 · 快速學習
透過 post-occupancy survey 確認使用者實際感受到的 thermal comfort，而不是只看設備或設計參數。此 optimization 強調 occupant satisfaction，目標是讓 substantial majority of building users 對熱環境感到可接受，並用定期調查形成管理回饋。
  • WELL Certification: 3 Pt | WELL Core: 3 Pt。
  • 適用 For All Spaces。
  • post-occupancy survey 需 administered at least twice a year。
  • 調查需 including once in June, July or August and once in December, January or February。
  • 調查時間需 at least six months after occupancy。
  • All regular occupants are invited to participate in the anonymous survey；Number of Regular Occupants More than 45 時，Minimum Number of Responses 為 35% of those regular occupants。

T03Thermal Zoning熱分區加分 Optimization
中文重點 · 快速學習
透過 thermal zoning 提升使用者對局部熱環境的控制能力，讓不同區域或不同人群可依需求調整溫度。考點在於控制方式可以是實體 thermostat 或 digital interface，且 thermal zone 大小有上限，避免一個控制區涵蓋過多使用者。
  • WELL Certification: 2 Pt | WELL Core: 3 Pt。
  • 適用 For All Spaces。
  • Part 1 要求 at least 90% of regularly occupied spaces 達成 thermostat control 條件。
  • Control over temperature 可透過 Thermostats present within the thermal zone 或 A digital interface accessible to occupants on a computer or phone。
  • The maximum size of each thermal zone is 650 ft²(60 m²) or 10 occupants, whichever is larger。
  • Projects earn points based on the number of thermal zones；One per 650 ft²(60 m²) 作為 zone 數量基準。

T04Individual Thermal Control個人熱控制加分 Optimization
中文重點 · 快速學習
讓 regular occupants 能依個人偏好進行 cooling、heating 或穿著調整，降低單一空調設定無法滿足所有人的問題。此 feature 的重點是個人層級的 thermal comfort devices 與 flexible dress code，且個人設備不得明顯改變其他人的熱環境。
  • Part 1: WELL Certification: 1 Pt | WELL Core: 0.5 Pt；適用 For All Spaces except Dwelling Units。
  • Personal cooling 可使用 user-adjustable thermostat、desk fan or ceiling fan、chair with mechanical cooling system，或 any other solution capable of affecting a PMV change of -0.5 within 15 minutes from activation。
  • 由 thermostat 控制的 room or thermal zone may not be regularly occupied by more than one person；風扇不得 increase air speed for other occupants。
  • Part 2: WELL Certification: 1 Pt | WELL Core: 0.5 Pt；適用 For All Spaces except Commercial Kitchen Spaces & Dwelling Units。
  • Personal heating 可使用 user-adjustable thermostat、Electric parabolic space heater、Electric heated chair or footwarmers、Blankets, which are washed at least weekly，或 any other solution capable of affecting a PMV change of +0.5 within 15 minutes from activation。
  • Part 3: WELL Certification: 1 Pt | WELL Core: 0.5 Pt；需有 flexible dress code policy，allows regular occupants to dress for individual thermal preferences。

T05Radiant Thermal Comfort輻射熱舒適加分 Optimization
中文重點 · 快速學習
使用 radiant heating 與 radiant cooling 系統，讓空間以輻射方式提供熱舒適，並降低完全依賴送風系統造成的限制。此 feature 關注 regularly occupied project area 中輻射系統的覆蓋比例，以及 radiant panels 是否真正覆蓋足夠牆面或天花面積。
  • Part 1: WELL Certification: 1 Pt | WELL Core: 2 Pt；適用 For All Spaces。
  • At least 50% of the regularly occupied project area is heated with radiant ceilings, walls or floors。
  • Radiant heating 也可使用 Radiant panels which cover at least half of the wall or ceiling to which they are attached；does not include steam radiators。
  • Part 2: WELL Certification: 1 Pt | WELL Core: 2 Pt；適用 For All Spaces。
  • At least 50% of the regularly occupied project area is cooled with radiant ceilings, walls or floors。
  • WELL Core Guidance: Meet these requirements in the whole building；Up to 10% of the total area occupied by tenants can be excluded from the feature scope。

T06Thermal Comfort Monitoring熱舒適監測加分 Optimization
中文重點 · 快速學習
透過 sensors 持續監測 thermal comfort parameters，讓 building managers 與使用者能及早發現不可接受的熱舒適狀態。此 feature 強調感測器位置、密度、量測頻率與 WELL Performance Verification 的 device requirements，避免監測資料不具代表性。
  • WELL Certification: 1 Pt | WELL Core: 0.5 Pt。
  • 適用 For All Spaces；Option 1: Thermal comfort monitors 需監測 dry-bulb temperature and relative humidity。
  • Sensors are located in occupiable areas；3.6-5.6 ft(1.1-1.7 m) above the floor。
  • Sensors 需 at least 3.3 ft(1 m) away from exterior walls, doors, direct sunlight, air supply/exhausts, mechanical fans, heaters or any other significant source of heat or cold。
  • A minimum of one sensor per 3,500 ft2(325 m2) of occupiable floor area。
  • Measurements are taken at least once every 15 minutes；Sensors comply with the Device Requirements listed in the WELL Performance Verification。
英文原文 standard reference
Intent 目的Monitor and effectively address unacceptable thermal comfort conditions and inform building managers and users of the thermal comfort parameters of their indoor environment.
